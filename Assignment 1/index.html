so each of us will reach the end
though different routes we take
we need go forward we must not
keep bowing do fate

and all mankind is on one sea

against the blind waves pounding
so let's sail on with this cracked mast
the harder the more wildly
though there's one night above us all
our separate stars give light

let's flap our wings to soar up high
let's flap them with all might
after each sunrise sunset comes
man gets his sleep in time
shine bright as long as you have chance
and may your flame not die
